Becoming a cutting-edge tech-company

I once told my professor that I'd become millionnaire when turning 30. 10 years later he called me. He had seen me on Forbes' 100 greatest fortunes review.

What matters is not what you or they think about your project. It's what you want. Even shitty ideas will get you somewhere if you work thoroughly and get the most of them. Of course there are tons of better, faster ideas. Yet they were only found by experience, or copied, adapted from somewhere else. To get there you have to know your business, and firsts you need to actually try and handle it, your way. While these advices, that I'd call guidelines do not apply in every field, they are for sure what made me jump so far off so fast in my early 20's when lauching ZCG with my crew. -BabinaX
